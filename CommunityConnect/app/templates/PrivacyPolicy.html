Privacy Policy

Community Connect is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

Collection of Personal Information (APP 1, 5)

We collect volunteer and organisation information to connect volunteers with suitable events. The information collected includes:

  • Volunteer names and contact details
  • Volunteer skills and availability
  • Organisation contact details and event information

When volunteers sign up, they are informed of the purpose of collecting their personal information and who will access it (APP 5).

Purpose and Use of Information (APP 6)

Collected information is only used for:

  • Matching volunteers with events based on their skills and preferences
  • Managing event registrations and sending relevant notifications

We do not share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes (APP 6).

Accuracy and Updating Information (APP 10)

Volunteers can update their personal details in their profiles to ensure information is accurate and complete. Organisations are verified when creating accounts to maintain accuracy.

Security of Information (APP 11)

We implement strong security measures, including secure password storage and restricted access, to protect personal information from misuse, loss, or unauthorised access.

Access and Correction (APP 12)

Volunteers can view, update, or delete their personal information at any time via their profile. Organisations can also update their account details. Requests for access or correction are honoured promptly.

Consent

By signing up as a volunteer or organisation, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information for the purposes described above, in line with the relevant APPs.

Contact Us

If you have questions or wish to access, correct, or delete your information, please contact us via your dashboard or email support@communityconnect.com.
