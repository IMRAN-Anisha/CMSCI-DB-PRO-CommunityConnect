Future Features & Enhancements:

    1. Video Demo:
        A short walkthrough showing the platform in action, highlighting registration, 
        event participation, and dashboard features.

    2. Complete Documentation:
        Full design documentation including ER diagrams, milestones, data dictionary,
        and explanations of design decisions.

    3. Social Worker & Professional Integration:
        Extend the platform beyond volunteers to allow private social workers, 
        coordinators, and administrators to manage community support services.

    4. Skill Matching & Recommendations:
        Automated matching between volunteer skills and event requirements,
        with suggestions for ideal event participation.

    5. Notifications & Messaging:
        Real-time notifications, reminders, and secure messaging between 
        volunteers, organisations, and social workers.

    6. Analytics Dashboard: 
        Insights into volunteer engagement, event participation, and impact
        metrics to support reporting and strategic planning.

    7. User Profiles & Portfolios: 
        Enhanced profiles including achievements, endorsements, certifications,
        and experience.

    8. Mobile-friendly Interface & App Integration:
        Optimisation for mobile devices and potential 
        future mobile app for easier accessibility.

    9. Privacy & Security Improvements:
        Advanced authentication, role-based access control,
        and GDPR-compliant data handling.

    10. Community Expansion:
        Support for additional user types, such as educators,
        NGOs, or local authorities, to foster a wider community network.

Long-term Vision:
Community Connect aims to become a hub for connecting skilled volunteers, social workers,
and organisations, facilitating community engagement, professional development, and social impact.

Important Note:
This platform acts purely as a connector for volunteers and support workers. 
All users are responsible for verifying credentials and conducting their own checks.
The platform is non-profit and does not supervise or employ any users.
